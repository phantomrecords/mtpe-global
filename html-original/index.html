About our club

Welcome to the Rock Mountain Cycling Club website; here you'll find all the info about mountain biking in the area and beyond. Our club was formed to maintain and expand mountain biking opportunities in our County. We are a volunteer organization and look for support from riders to help with trail work, advocacy and to be good stewards of the land.
